# VALENS merch — mockup style guide

Consistent across ALL images:
- Photoreal 3D product render, studio product-photography lighting.
- Item rendered in VALENS violet (#8465D6 family — deep purple through light-violet accents), with a small embroidered/printed VALENS mark (simple mountain-peak "V" wordmark) placed subtly on the product.
- Dark studio background (near-black, #0C080D–#171019), soft gradient vignette.
- Soft floor/contact shadow beneath the product; no props, no hands, no models.
- Centred composition, square 1:1 crop, no text, no watermark, no logo overlays besides the VALENS mark on the product itself.

Export as PNG named exactly `<sku>.png` into `assets/merch/`.
