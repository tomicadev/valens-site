# VALENS merch — mockup style guide

Consistent across ALL images:
- Photoreal 3D product render, studio product-photography lighting.
- **Garment/product base colour: near-black charcoal (#0C080D–#14101A family — the
  same near-black as the VALENS logo's background), NOT violet.** The product should
  read as a distinct dark charcoal against the pure-black backdrop — subtle fabric
  sheen/texture separates it from the background, not colour contrast.
- **The VALENS mark is the ONLY spot of colour** — a small "V" (mountain-peak wordmark)
  in its exact two-tone gradient: left leg deep purple (#6F4DB3), right leg light violet
  (#9B78D4), meeting in a point at the base. Placed subtly on the product (embroidered
  tag, printed chest hit, stitched loop, etc. — small, not a giant chest print).
- **Use the exact attached/reference VALENS "V" logo as-is — do NOT redraw, restyle,
  reinterpret, or generate a new/similar "V" mark.** AI image models tend to invent a
  lookalike logo instead of reproducing the real one; if the tool supports a reference/
  logo-upload input, feed it the real mark and lock it in place rather than describing
  it from scratch. If it must be described, reproduce it exactly: two flat, straight-edged
  triangular strokes meeting at a sharp point at the bottom, no rounded corners, no
  outline, no drop shadow, no 3D bevel — a flat two-tone geometric mark, not a photoreal
  emblem.
- **Scene background matches the product**: near-black (#0C080D), same tone family as
  the garment, soft gradient vignette so the product still reads as a separate object
  under studio light (not colour, just soft shadow/highlight falloff).
- Soft floor/contact shadow beneath the product; no props, no hands, no models.
- Centred composition, square 1:1 crop, no text, no watermark, no logo overlays besides
  the single VALENS mark on the product itself.

Export as PNG named exactly `<sku>.png` into `assets/merch/`.
