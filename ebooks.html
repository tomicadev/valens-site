Our most demanding flagship — engineered set by set.

  • 32 exercises, ~108 challenging sets per week

  • Includes the Training Foundations handbook

  • 5-day Upper/Lower/Push/Pull/Legs split

  • 5-day split, technique-first
  • 4-week foundation block, then deload

Apex PPL — 6-Day Push/Pull/Legs

Every muscle, twice a week, for relentless size.

€29

A 6-day push/pull/legs split for steady size and strength. Every muscle is trained twice a week; the A sessions open heavy, the B sessions open with a different lift and rep range. Every set carries a target rep count and an RIR cue — you bring the working weight, the program guides the effort.

  • 6-day Push/Pull/Legs, run twice a week
  • 30 exercises, ~112 sets per week
  • A/B sessions vary the lifts and rep ranges
  • Hypertrophy built on a strength base
  • Includes the Training Foundations handbook
  • Built to log along in the VALENS app

Three sessions. Three lifts. Five sets of five.

€29

A 3-day strength block centred on 5×5 work on the squat, bench and deadlift, backed by targeted accessories. Tight RIR targets on the main lifts build raw strength; you add weight as you progress, and the reps and effort are mapped for you.

  • 3-day full-body strength block
  • 5×5 on squat, bench and deadlift
  • 13 focused exercises, tight RIR on the mains
  • Add weight as you progress
  • Includes the Training Foundations handbook
  • Built to log along in the VALENS app

PHUL — Power & Hypertrophy

Two power days, two hypertrophy days, one strong week.

€29

A 4-day Power/Hypertrophy Upper-Lower program: two heavier power days to drive strength, two higher-volume hypertrophy days to drive size. Power days run tighter and heavier; hypertrophy days chase the pump. You bring the weight.

  • 4-day Upper/Lower, Power + Hypertrophy
  • 2 power days plus 2 hypertrophy days
  • 21 exercises — strength and size in one week
  • Heavier power work, higher-volume pump work
  • Includes the Training Foundations handbook
  • Built to log along in the VALENS app
